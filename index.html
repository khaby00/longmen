The Legend

In ancient China, the Yellow River carved its way through mountains and plains. Among its restless currents swam countless carps, their scales flashing like sunlight. For generations they whispered a single dream: the Leap of Longmen.

At the Dragon Gate, a thunderous waterfall roared. The legend said that any carp brave enough to leap those gates would be transformed by heaven into a dragon — a creature of wisdom, strength, and freedom. Many attempted the leap; many failed. Yet hope endured, and brave souls kept trying.

One day, against all odds, a carp rose above the torrent. It cut through spray and sky, and when the water fell away it no longer swam but flew. The carp had become a dragon, radiant and unstoppable.

This metamorphosis is our guiding story: together we leap, together we rise, together we become.
