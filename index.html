A Story Written in Fire

For centuries, the Chinese have known the tale: a humble carp swims upstream, facing endless struggle. At the final gate, the Longmen waterfall, those who dare to leap are reborn as dragons.

It is not just a myth. It is a metaphor for ambition, for courage, for the impossible transformation every human dreams of. To rise above the ordinary. To become more than what the world expects.

But here is the truth: while the West has Doge and Pepe, China — with its greatest legend — has nothing. No carp. No dragon. No meme that speaks to its billion voices.
